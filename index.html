The IWW is a union for all workers. We are a dedicated labor organization fighting for workers across all industries. Any worker can join to help organize in our workplaces, and in our communities.

Founded in 1905, the IWW works towards a better society based on worker control, and economic democracy. The IWW is dedicated to the values laid out in our preamble.
